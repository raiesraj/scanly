Privacy Policy — SnapQR
privacy and policy
SnapQR – QR & Barcode Scanner · Updated June 2026
In plain words

SnapQR reads QR codes and barcodes using your camera and helps you create new ones. It is built to keep your data on your device — we collect as little as possible.

Your camera

The camera is used only while the scanner is open, to detect QR codes in real time. Frames are processed on your phone and are never recorded, stored, or uploaded.

On-device history

Scans and codes you create are saved locally in this app so you can find them later. This history lives on your device and you can clear it anytime from Settings.

What we collect

Nothing that identifies you. We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage counts (such as how often the app opens) to improve performance. No scan contents are ever included.

Opening links

When you choose to open a scanned link, your device hands it to your browser or the relevant app. Scanly does not track where you go after that.

Third parties

We do not sell or share your data. We have no advertising trackers. The only network requests SnapQR makes are anonymous diagnostics, which you can disable.

Your choices

You control camera access, history, and diagnostics in Settings. Removing the app deletes all locally stored history permanently.
